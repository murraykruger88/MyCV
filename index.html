Experience & Skills

I am a freelancer across various creative industries. As a Set Dresser (2016-2021) Film/TV/Commercials /Music Videos (with Production Designer Chris Joubert). Sales at HoodLinkd. As an arts writer, Kruger's reviews have been published by the likes of www.artthrob.co.za, The Mail & Guardian and The Sunday Independent between 2010 and 2013 with forthcoming reviews in 2022. He has provided data capturing/analysis services to Corrigall & Co in 2019-2022. Prior to this Kruger has worked as an artist between 2009 and 2016. His work has been exhibited both internationally (Switzerland, La Reunion, Germany, France) and locally. Notable examples of the latter include participation in exhibitions at The Cape 09 Biennale, The Standard Bank Art Gallery, The Goodman Gallery (CPT and JHB), The Joburg Art Fair and The Cape Town Art Fair. As a curator and curatorial assistant Kruger has been involved in a selection of projects between 2013 and 2014 at The Wits Art Museum, The Johannesburg Art Gallery, The Wits Substation, The Bag Factory Artists’ Studios and South African Fashion Week.
